Signs You Need Immediate Gas Line Repair

  • Unusual Odors : A potent sulfur or rotten egg smell can indicate a gas leak.
  • Hissing Sounds : Audible hissing near gas lines suggests a potentially hazardous leak.
  • Dead Vegetation : Unexplained wilting or dead greenery around your gas line can be a sign of leakage.
